I am a Data Science PhD student at the [Halicioglu Data Science Institute](https://datascience.ucsd.edu/), University of California, San Diego, focused on the intersection of genomics and machine learning.

My research involves developing biologically informed models, including evolution-guided tokenization for DNA language models (EvoLen) and integrated genetic risk prediction for complex diseases like asthma. I am also interested in designing long-context LLM architectures with memory banks for continuous learning and cross-domain adaptability.

Prior to my PhD, I earned a B.S. in Data Science, Mathematics, and Economics from UC San Diego, graduating with Provost Honors in March 2025.
